# Project Brief

## Business Interpretation

This company delivers end-to-end data warehouse construction services for business customers. Work may include solution design, material supply, engineering coordination, labor organization, and final project delivery.

The commercial model is project-based B2B sales rather than commodity sales. Deals are likely to involve multiple meetings, requirement clarification, formal quotations, contract review, and a structured handoff into delivery.

## CRM Direction

This CRM should support the full sales-to-delivery lifecycle:

- capture and qualify leads,
- maintain company and contact records,
- manage opportunities over a realistic B2B pipeline,
- produce quotations,
- convert successful business into contracts,
- hand off won deals into delivery projects.

## Product Positioning

The system should behave like a practical internal business CRM for project delivery companies. It should prioritize operational clarity, continuity across stages, and maintainable workflows over flashy demos or excessive abstraction.
